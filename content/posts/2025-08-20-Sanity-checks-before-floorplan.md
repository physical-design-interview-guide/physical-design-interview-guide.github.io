Inputs:</b> No gate input pins should be left unconnected (floating)<o:p></o:p></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 75.95pt; mso-list: l0 level3 lfo1; tab-stops: list 75.95pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: Wingdings; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: Wingdings; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: Wingdings;">ยง<span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; font-feature-settings: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-size-adjust: none; font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; line-height: normal;">&nbsp; </span></span><!--[endif]--><b>Multi-Driven

Loops:</b> Check for unintended feedback loops in combinational logic.<o:p></o:p></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left: 18.0pt; mso-add-space: auto; mso-list: l1 level1 lfo2; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;">o<span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; font-feature-settings: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-size-adjust: none; font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; line-height: normal;">&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span><!--[endif]--><b>Design Structure:</b> <o:p></o:p></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 39.95pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo1; tab-stops: list 39.95pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;">o<span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; font-feature-settings: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-size-adjust: none; font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; line-height: normal;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><!--[endif]--><b>Empty
Modules/Black Boxes:</b> Identify any modules referenced in the netlist but
lacking a definition (black boxes) or empty modules. Ensure these are expected
(e.g., hard macros, analog blocks) and corresponding models/LEF exist.<o:p></o:p></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 39.95pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo1; tab-stops: list 39.95pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;">o<span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; font-feature-settings: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-size-adjust: none; font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; line-height: normal;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><!--[endif]--><b>Unresolved
References:</b> Similar to black boxes, ensure all cell instantiations have
corresponding definitions in the libraries.<o:p></o:p></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 39.95pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo1; tab-stops: list 39.95pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;">o<span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; font-feature-settings: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-size-adjust: none; font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; line-height: normal;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><!--[endif]--><b>Assign
Statements:</b> Netlists should generally not contain continuous assignment
(assign) statements; these should be synthesized into gates.<o:p></o:p></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left: 18.0pt; mso-add-space: auto; mso-list: l1 level1 lfo2; tab-stops: 42.55pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;">o<span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; font-feature-settings: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-size-adjust: none; font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; line-height: normal;">&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span><!--[endif]--><b>Constraints Checks (SDC - Synopsys Design
Constraints):</b> <o:p></o:p></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 39.95pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo1; tab-stops: list 39.95pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;">o<span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; font-feature-settings: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-size-adjust: none; font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; line-height: normal;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><!--[endif]--><b>Clock
Definitions:</b> Ensure all clocks are defined (create_clock,
create_generated_clock) and reach all sequential elements (no unclocked
registers).<o:p></o:p></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 39.95pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo1; tab-stops: list 39.95pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;">o<span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; font-feature-settings: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-size-adjust: none; font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; line-height: normal;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><!--[endif]--><b>I/O
Delays:</b> Check if input (set_input_delay) and output (set_output_delay)
delays are defined for all primary I/O ports involved in timing paths.<o:p></o:p></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 39.95pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo1; tab-stops: list 39.95pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;">o<span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; font-feature-settings: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-size-adjust: none; font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; line-height: normal;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><!--[endif]--><b>DRC
Constraints:</b> Verify that design rule constraints like set_max_transition,
set_max_capacitance, set_max_fanout are appropriately defined.<o:p></o:p></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 39.95pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo1; tab-stops: list 39.95pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;">o<span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; font-feature-settings: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-size-adjust: none; font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; line-height: normal;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><!--[endif]--><b>Unconstrained
Paths/Endpoints:</b> Identify any timing paths or endpoints that are not
covered by existing constraints (using check_timing). These might need explicit
constraints or could be intended false paths.<o:p></o:p></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 39.95pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo1; tab-stops: list 39.95pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;">o<span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; font-feature-settings: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-size-adjust: none; font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; line-height: normal;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><!--[endif]--><b>Multi-Driven
Clocks:</b> Check for registers driven by multiple clocks, which usually
indicates an error in the SDC or design.<o:p></o:p></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left: 18.0pt; mso-add-space: auto; mso-list: l1 level1 lfo2; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;">o<span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; font-feature-settings: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-size-adjust: none; font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; line-height: normal;">&nbsp;&nbsp;
