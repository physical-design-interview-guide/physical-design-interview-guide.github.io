<p>&nbsp;<span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; text-indent: -18pt;">o<span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; font-feature-settings: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-size-adjust: none; font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; line-height: normal;">&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><b style="text-indent: -18pt;">Target
Utilization at Start:</b><span style="text-indent: -18pt;"> The initial target core utilization for PnR typically
ranges from </span><b style="text-indent: -18pt;">50% to 70%</b><span style="text-indent: -18pt;">.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 57.8pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo1; tab-stops: list 57.8pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">o<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]-->Lower
utilization (e.g., 50-60%) provides more whitespace, making routing easier,
reducing congestion, potentially improving timing (less detour), and offering
more flexibility for CTS and ECOs. This is often preferred for high-performance
designs or designs with known congestion challenges.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 57.8pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo1; tab-stops: list 57.8pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">o<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]-->Higher
utilization (e.g., 65-70%, sometimes even higher for specific blocks) aims to
minimize die area (cost). However, it increases the risk of congestion, may
make timing closure harder, and leaves less room for post-route optimizations
and ECOs.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 18.0pt; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1; tab-stops: list 18.0pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">o<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]--><b>70%
vs 50%:</b> The choice depends heavily on design goals and characteristics: <o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 57.8pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo1; tab-stops: list 57.8pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">o<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]--><b>Choose
50% if:</b> <o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 90.0pt; mso-list: l0 level3 lfo1; tab-stops: list 90.0pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: Wingdings; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: Wingdings; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: Wingdings;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">§<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]-->High
performance/frequency is the primary goal.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 90.0pt; mso-list: l0 level3 lfo1; tab-stops: list 90.0pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: Wingdings; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: Wingdings; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: Wingdings;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">§<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]-->The
design has complex routing or known congestion hotspots.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 90.0pt; mso-list: l0 level3 lfo1; tab-stops: list 90.0pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: Wingdings; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: Wingdings; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: Wingdings;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">§<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]-->Significant
CTS overhead or late-stage ECOs are anticipated.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 90.0pt; mso-list: l0 level3 lfo1; tab-stops: list 90.0pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: Wingdings; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: Wingdings; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: Wingdings;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">§<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]-->Power
density / thermal concerns require spreading cells out.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 90.0pt; mso-list: l0 level3 lfo1; tab-stops: list 90.0pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: Wingdings; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: Wingdings; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: Wingdings;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">§<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]-->Using
an advanced node with restrictive routing rules.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 57.8pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo1; tab-stops: list 57.8pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">o<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]--><b>Choose
70% if:</b> <o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 90.0pt; mso-list: l0 level3 lfo1; tab-stops: list 90.0pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: Wingdings; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: Wingdings; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: Wingdings;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">§<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]-->Area/cost
minimization is the primary goal.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 90.0pt; mso-list: l0 level3 lfo1; tab-stops: list 90.0pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: Wingdings; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: Wingdings; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: Wingdings;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">§<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]-->The
design is relatively simple with moderate performance targets.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 90.0pt; mso-list: l0 level3 lfo1; tab-stops: list 90.0pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: Wingdings; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: Wingdings; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: Wingdings;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">§<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]-->Congestion
is not expected to be a major issue.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 90.0pt; mso-list: l0 level3 lfo1; tab-stops: list 90.0pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: Wingdings; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: Wingdings; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: Wingdings;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">§<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]-->Minimal
ECOs are expected.<o:p></o:p></p>
