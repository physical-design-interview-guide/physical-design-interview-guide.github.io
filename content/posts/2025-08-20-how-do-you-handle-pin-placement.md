<p>&nbsp;<span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; text-indent: -18pt;">o<span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; font-feature-settings: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-size-adjust: none; font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; line-height: normal;">&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span style="text-indent: -18pt;">Minimize
wire length between pins and the internal logic/macros they connect to.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 18.0pt; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1; tab-stops: list 18.0pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">o<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]-->Reduce
routing congestion, especially near the block boundary.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 18.0pt; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1; tab-stops: list 18.0pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">o<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]-->Align
pins logically with connected blocks at the next level of hierarchy.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 18.0pt; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1; tab-stops: list 18.0pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">o<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]-->Meet
timing requirements for critical interface paths.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 18.0pt; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1; tab-stops: list 18.0pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">o<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]-->Ensure
routability and avoid pin access issues.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 18.0pt; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1; tab-stops: list 18.0pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">o<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]-->Group
related signals (e.g., buses) together logically.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 18.0pt;"><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></p>

<p class="MsoListParagraphCxSpFirst" style="margin-left: 18.0pt; mso-add-space: auto; mso-list: l1 level1 lfo3; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">o<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span></span><!--[endif]--><b>Understand Connectivity:</b> Analyze which
internal blocks/macros connect to which I/O pins (using flylines or
schematics). Understand connectivity to external blocks at the top level.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoListParagraphCxSpMiddle" style="margin-left: 18.0pt; mso-add-space: auto; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo2; tab-stops: list 18.0pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">o<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span></span><!--[endif]--><b>Consider Top-Level Context:</b> If the block
is part of a larger design, align pin locations with the corresponding
connections on adjacent blocks or routing channels at the top level to minimize
top-level routing detours.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoListParagraphCxSpLast" style="margin-left: 18.0pt; mso-add-space: auto; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo2; tab-stops: list 18.0pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">o<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span></span><!--[endif]--><b>Tool Assistance:</b> PnR tools provide
features for pin placement: <o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 57.8pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo2; tab-stops: list 57.8pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">o<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]-->Automatic
placement based on connectivity ("snap pins" or similar).<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 57.8pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo2; tab-stops: list 57.8pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">o<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]-->Spreading
algorithms to distribute pins evenly along edges.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 57.8pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo2; tab-stops: list 57.8pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">o<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]-->Manual
placement via GUI or coordinates.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 57.8pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo2; tab-stops: list 57.8pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">o<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]-->Pin
editors for detailed ordering and spacing.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left: 18.0pt; mso-add-space: auto; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo2; tab-stops: list 18.0pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">o<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span></span><!--[endif]--><b>Refinement &amp; Optimization with iterations
based on congestion and timing reports.</b> <o:p></o:p></p>
