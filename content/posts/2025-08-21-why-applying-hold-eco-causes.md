<p><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; text-indent: -18pt;">o<span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; font-feature-settings: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-size-adjust: none; font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; line-height: normal;">&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span><span style="text-indent: -18pt;">A "cell cannot be legalized" error
during an ECO (especially a hold ECO, which often involves adding buffers)
means the PnR tool cannot find a valid, DRC-clean placement location for the
newly added or modified cells that aligns with the site rows and doesn't
overlap with other cells.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 54.0pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo1; tab-stops: list 54.0pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">o<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]--><b>High
Cell Density/Congestion:</b> There's simply no physical space (empty sites) to
place the new buffer without causing overlaps.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 54.0pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo1; tab-stops: list 54.0pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">o<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]--><b>Filler
cells not removed from the design.</b><o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoListParagraphCxSpFirst" style="margin-left: 54.0pt; mso-add-space: auto; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo1; tab-stops: list 54.0pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">o<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]--><b>Frozen
Placement:</b> If too many surrounding cells are marked as fixed or have
dont_touch attributes, the legalizer has very little flexibility to move
existing cells to make space for the ECO cell.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoListParagraphCxSpLast" style="margin-left: 54.0pt; mso-add-space: auto; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo1; tab-stops: list 54.0pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">o<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]--><b>Large
ECO Cell Size:</b> If the ECO involves adding a large buffer or ECO is adding
so many buffers in certain area causing placement issues.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 54.0pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo1; tab-stops: list 54.0pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">o<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]--><b>Row
Utilization Limits:</b> Some tools might have constraints on maximum row
utilization that prevent adding more cells even if a few empty sites seem
available.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 54.0pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo1; tab-stops: list 54.0pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">o<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]--><b>Multi-Height
Cells:</b> If the ECO cell is a multi-height cell and the required space
pattern (e.g., two adjacent free rows) isn't available.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 54.0pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo1; tab-stops: list 54.0pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">o<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]--><b>Power
Domain Constraints:</b> If the ECO cell needs to be in a specific power domain,
and that domain's physical voltage area is full.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 18.0pt; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1; tab-stops: list 18.0pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">o<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]--><b>What
Can Be Done?</b><o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 54.0pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo1; tab-stops: list 54.0pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">o<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]-->Check
for Filler or placement blockage issue, this is easy to resolve. By deleting.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 54.0pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo1; tab-stops: list 54.0pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">o<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]--><b>Unfix
Nearby Cells Temporarily:</b> or manual movement to fix placement issue.<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 54.0pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo1; tab-stops: list 54.0pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">o<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]--><b>Use
Spare Cells (If Available and Pre-placed):</b> If spare cells using too much
space, can we reduce it if allowed?<o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 54.0pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo1; tab-stops: list 54.0pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">o<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]--><b>Increase
cell shift distance limit so tool can move cell to further distance and resolve
legalisation.</b><o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 54.0pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo1; tab-stops: list 54.0pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">o<span style="font: 7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><!--[endif]-->Check
ECO quality if ECO is simply too large. <o:p></o:p></p>
