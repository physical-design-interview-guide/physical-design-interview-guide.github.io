Integrity (SI):</b> Quality of an electrical signal as it travels from a driver
to a receiver through an interconnect. Major SI concerns include: <o:p></o:p></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 54.15pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo1; tab-stops: list 54.15pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;">o<span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; font-feature-settings: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-size-adjust: none; font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; line-height: normal;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><!--[endif]--><b>Crosstalk:</b>
Unwanted coupling between adjacent signal nets.<o:p></o:p></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 54.15pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo1; tab-stops: list 54.15pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;">o<span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; font-feature-settings: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-size-adjust: none; font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; line-height: normal;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><!--[endif]--><b>IR
Drop:</b> Voltage drop on the power/ground network affecting cell performance.<o:p></o:p></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 54.15pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo1; tab-stops: list 54.15pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;">o<span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; font-feature-settings: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-size-adjust: none; font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; line-height: normal;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><!--[endif]--><b>Electromigration
(EM):</b> Reliability issue due to metal migration under high current density.<o:p></o:p></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 54.15pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo1; tab-stops: list 54.15pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;">o<span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; font-feature-settings: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-size-adjust: none; font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; line-height: normal;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><!--[endif]--><b>Timing
Jitter/Phase Noise:</b> Variations in the timing of signal edges, especially
critical for clocks.<o:p></o:p></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 18.15pt; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1; tab-stops: list 18.15pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;">o<span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; font-feature-settings: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-size-adjust: none; font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; line-height: normal;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><!--[endif]--><b>Crosstalk
Noise/Glitch:</b> It occurs when a switching signal on one net (the <b>aggressor</b>)
induces a voltage disturbance (a noise bump or "glitch") on a nearby
net (the <b>victim</b>) that is supposed to be holding a constant logic level
(static). <o:p></o:p></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 54.15pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo1; tab-stops: list 54.15pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;">o<span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; font-feature-settings: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-size-adjust: none; font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; line-height: normal;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><!--[endif]--><b>Mechanism:</b>
The coupling capacitance (Ccâ€‹) between the aggressor and victim nets allows the
rapid voltage change (dV/dt) on the aggressor to inject current onto the victim
net, causing its voltage to temporarily deviate from its steady VDD or VSS
level.<o:p></o:p></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 54.15pt; mso-list: l0 level2 lfo1; tab-stops: list 54.15pt; text-indent: -18.0pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;; font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-size: 11.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Courier New&quot;;">o<span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; font-feature-settings: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-size-adjust: none; font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-emoji: normal; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; line-height: normal;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><!--[endif]--><b>Impact:</b>
If the induced voltage glitch is large enough (exceeds the noise margin of the
receiving gate on the victim net) and lasts long enough, it can cause the
receiving gate to momentarily interpret the wrong logic level, leading to
functional failure. Even smaller glitches can increase cell power consumption
